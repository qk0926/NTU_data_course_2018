病史(Brief History) 
This is a 60-year-old man with:
1. Coronary artery diseases, left main 80%stenosis and 3 vessel disease
2. Congestive heart failure , NYHA III(EF:14.8%)
3. Type 2 diabetes mellitus
4. Essential hypertension

He had history of frequent hospitalization due to chest tightness induced by ischemic heart disease. The symptom as chest tightness developed over 10 years, but the frequency increased in recent 1 year. Initially, the symtom could be resolved by NTG and rest, but became poor response in the end. According to his latest study, cardiac catheterization on 7/8 revealed CAD, 3VD+LM; LM: distal stenosis 80 %; LAD: proximal stenosis 70 %, middle stenosis 70 %, D1 stenosis 80 %; LCX: diffuse stenosis from middle to distal up to 90%; RCA: proximal stenosis 80 %, middle stenosis 70 %, distal stenosis 70 %, PDA stenosis 80 %. Echocardiography on 2016/9/10 revealed left ventricular systolic function is severely reduced. LVEF: 14.8, LVIDd: 6.8 cm, LVIDs: 6.4 cm were also recorded. Upon MRI on 2016/9/19, severely reduced LV function with LVEF: 16.7%, marked global hypokinesis of LV, and focal subendocardial delayed hyperenhancement at apical inferior wall of LV myocardium were noted. He refused surgery intervention and chose to use medical control initially, but chose to accepted surgery as CABG in the end. The surgery was arranged next week. However, sudden onset of collapse was noted this morming around 09:00 on 2016/10/8. He was sent to other hospital's ER, DC shock X 2(VT ) was carried out. He was then return of spontaneous circulation under dopamine usage. He was then referred to our hospital under his family's request. Therefore, he was admitted to our ward for further management.
