病史(Brief History) 
The patient is a 58-year-old man with unknown neuromuscular disease, with quadriparesis, and poorly-controlled diabetes mellitus and hypertension. He was confined to bed but ADL-independent since left foot fracture after traffic accident 1 year ago. Family reported occasional urinary and fecal incontinence after foot fracture.

One month ago, he developed quadriparesis (fell at home) without loss of consciousness, cognitive dysfunction, or new sensory deficit (baseline paresthesia and numbness, suspected diabetic neuropathy). Intermittent muscle weakness of all 4 limbs was noted: at best, patient could feed himself, raise his limbs, but no antigravity movement at times. He was worked up for neurological deficit at 雙和 Hospital, but no diagnosis was reached. Due to ADL dependence, he was admitted to nursing home after discharge, since 2015-09-18.

Fever, productive cough, and muscle soreness were noted on 2015-10-02 at nursing home. Due to persistent symptoms, he was taken to 博仁 Hospital, where healthcare-associated pneumonia was favored due to infiltrates found via chest X-ray. Urinalysis showed no pyuria. Empirical ceftazidime (1000mg Q12H IV) was prescribed, after which fever rapidly subsided. Follow-up chest X-ray also showed improvement of bilateral lung infiltrates. However, the patient developed a "heavy sensation" over chest ("覺得被子蓋太厚" when no blankets were over his torso) on 2015-10-08, without mention of dyspnea, breathlessness, or desaturation.

Loss of consciousness, bradycardia (<30bpm), and absence of blood pressure were found at 04:40 on 2015-10-09, status post cardiopulmonary resuscitation and intubation. Initial ABG disclosed respiratory acidosis (pH: 7.110, pCO2: 74.1mmHg, pO2: 73.1mmHg, HCO3: 23.7mEq/L). Return of spontaneous circulation was achieved after 14 minutes. No elevation of cardiac enzymes or electrolyte imbalance were found after CPR. However, the patient remained comatose after ROSC (E1VtM1), with seizures. Brain CT on 2015-10-10 disclosed only subgaleal hematoma and age-related atrophic changes in bilateral brain parenchyma. Family decided to seek second opinion at NTUH ER, thus he was admitted to NTUH 3C1 medical ICU on 2015-10-12 for workup and care.
